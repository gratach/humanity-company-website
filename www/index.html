Company Owned By Humanity

A democracy experiment.

We are living in a world that is highly influenced by large companies whose goals are not always aligned perfectly with the public interest. Wouldn't it be nice if there were companies that are owned by humanity in the sense that they are managed by democratic decisions where every person in the world has one equally weighted vote?

This project aims to make this vision a reality. We want to start by building a small community of people who are interested in experimenting with new forms of democracy. We want to work on developing and testing new tools and methods that improve cooperation between people and enable them to achieve common goals.

Note: We are in the early stages of development and are looking for people who are interested in joining us on this journey.
